Computer Laboratory 4

Located at the second floor of CICT Building. This computer laboratory is equipped with 15 units Asus BM6330 and 14 units Acer Veriton M2610G installed computers that are connected to network hub/switch, with printer, a room with an air-conditioner, a ceiling fan, and a set of fire extinguisher mounted on the wall. A whiteboard is installed inside the room.
